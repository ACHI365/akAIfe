Fun Fact #1: Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible!
Fun Fact #2: Octopuses have three hearts. Two pump blood through the gills, while the third pumps it through the rest of the body.
Fun Fact #3: The shortest war in history was between Britain and Zanzibar in 1896. It lasted only 38 minutes before Zanzibar surrendered.
Fun Fact #4: A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus. It takes 243 Earth days for Venus to rotate once on its axis, but only 225 Earth days to complete one orbit around the Sun.
Fun Fact #5: Cows have best friends and can become stressed when separated from them. They form strong social bonds with other cows in their herd.
Fun Fact #6: The first commercial flight took place in 1914 and consisted of a 23-minute journey across Tampa Bay, Florida. The fare was $5 for the single passenger.
Fun Fact #7: The fingerprints of koalas are so similar to humans that they have occasionally been confused at crime scenes!
Fun Fact #8: There are more possible iterations of a game of chess than there are atoms in the known universe. The number of possible unique chess games is estimated to be around 10^120.
Fun Fact #9: The Great Barrier Reef is the largest living structure on Earth. It can be seen from outer space and stretches over 1,400 miles.
Fun Fact #10: In Switzerland, it's illegal to own just one guinea pig because they're social animals and can become depressed if kept alone.
